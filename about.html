Our History

TPS Staffing started in 2003 focusing on provided agile staffing solutions to businesses. Formerly, Staffing Plus, the name was changed to accommodate a corporate approach to staffing. Currently, TPS Staffing services top 500 corporations and business. Our main focus is to provide a solution which allows businesses increase revenue through maintaining employees and low overhead.

Demographics

TPS Staffing services top 500 corporations and businesses with their professional staffing needs.

Stats

Approximately 55% of companies and 74% of staffing agencies are in the temporary and contract staffing sector of the industry.

More information to consider:

  • Half (49%) of staffing employees say it’s a way to get a permanent job.
  • Nine out 10 said staffing work made them more employable.
  • One-third (35%) were offered a permanent job by a client where they worked on an assignment, and two-thirds (66%) of those accepted the offers of permanent employment.
  • Most (76%) work full time, comparable to the overall workforce (82%).
